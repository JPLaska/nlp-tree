TIGER Corpus Sampler

(200 corpus graphs)

1. General information

Name: TIGER Corpus Sampler
ID: TIGERSampler
Format: NeGra format, version 3
Author: The TIGER Project
Date: May 2003
Description: A sampler of 200 sentences of the TIGER Corpus. Comprises morphological and lemma annotation. Also comprises a collection of several example queries. With kind permission of the TIGER Project.

2. Corpus details

Features (T): word, pos, morph, lemma
Features (NT): cat
Labelled edges: yes
Crossing edges: yes
Secondary edges: yes
